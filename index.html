How it works

AI reads your profile — your mission, projects, goals, criteria — and analyzes the task in your specific context. Not abstract advice. Advice for you.

Then it pulls from your mentor base — referencing what Reed Hoffman, Маргулан, Kanye, or Moebius would say about this specific task in your situation.

Kaizen Filter
✓ WANT

Your mission. Your dream. Your ikigai. Gives energy.

⚠ MUST

Someone else's want. Obligation. Drains energy.

Quick Tips
→ Be specific. "Send email to PopMart" beats "work on toys"
→ One task at a time. Not "do marketing"
→ Include context: deadline, who's involved, why now
